In celebration of the cultural richness of Sri Lanka, our company, Pettah AI, introduces a unique project merging technology with tradition: "Pettah AI Wesak." This innovative endeavor aims to revive and preserve the essence of Sri Lankan folk tales through the power of artificial intelligence. Traditional Sri Lankan folk tales, or "පන්සිය පනස් ජාතක කතා," are a treasure trove of wisdom, morality, and entertainment deeply rooted in the island's heritage. However, with the passage of time, many of these stories risk being forgotten or fading into obscurity.
